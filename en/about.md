I am Carlo Scianatico.

This site does not exist to make what I do look bigger than it is. It exists for the opposite reason: **to make what I am actually building readable in an orderly way**.

The areas that interest me most are three.

## Economic thinking

I want to understand how incentives work, how decision-making power is distributed, and why some systems produce value while others waste resources and energy.

## Applied artificial intelligence

I use AI as an operational lever. I care about it when it helps structure processes, clarify problems, accelerate analysis, and turn intuitions into prototypes.

## Building projects

I am working on entrepreneurial projects and on a professional identity that combines reasoning, execution, and the ability to write clearly.

In short: I care about **building substance** and making it visible without fluff, poses, or self-celebration.
